# What's New in Bublr

## What we had before
- A calm, ad-free space to write
- Dark and light mode
- Simple profiles
- An API for developers

---

## What's new

### Talk to write
Hit record and speak your thoughts. Bublr transcribes them for you—perfect for when typing feels like too much.

### Your own corner of the internet
Use your own domain (like yourname.com) for just $2/month. Your profile, your brand, your space.

### Built-in newsletters
Readers can subscribe to you. When you publish, they get an email. No extra tools needed.

### Follow your favorite writers
Follow people. Get notified when they post. See who's following you.

### Profiles that feel like you
Add your skills, social links, bio, and custom sections. Choose what to show and how to show it. Add your own footer branding.

### Color your posts
Pick a dot color for each post—make your profile page uniquely yours.

### Import from Medium
Leaving Medium? Bring your articles with you in one click.

### Save for later
Bookmark posts to your reading list and come back when you're ready.

### See who's reading
View counts on every post so you know what's resonating.

### Smarter search
Find posts and writers faster with improved search.

### Better for discovery
Your posts are now optimized to show up in search engines and AI assistants.

### Developer API
Generate API keys and build on top of Bublr programmatically.

### Safe space
AI moderation keeps spam and harmful content out automatically.
